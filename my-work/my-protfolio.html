Welcome to my portfolio site! Here you can see everything I’ve worked on so far in the world of design and front-end development. This site is my personal project, and also a showcase of my skills in creating modern, responsive websites. Using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern technologies like React.js and GSAP animations, I’ve created a site that is easy to navigate and visually appealing. Here, you won't just see my projects, but also experience how I approached the design and development of each one.
For building my projects, I used **Vite** as the bundler, which allows for faster development and a more efficient build process. For animations, I rely on **GSAP**, a powerful library that helps me create smooth and engaging transitions. To enhance the user experience, I integrated **BarbaJS** for seamless page transitions, giving the site a smooth, single-page application feel. The website is fully responsive, ensuring it looks and functions well on any device, from mobile phones to desktops. I also followed best practices for **SEO** to ensure optimal visibility and performance in search engines, helping users easily find my work.
